About

Hi, I'm Eric. I grew up in a construction family, so tools and job sites were my playground. That upbringing turned into an obsession with learning how things work, and more importantly, how to fix them when they don't.

The name Curio comes from curiosity, "a strong desire to know or learn something." That sums me up pretty well. Over the years that curiosity has taken me many places: property management, software engineering, farming, salmon processing, painting, drafting, tiny house design and construction, web development, and more.

The common thread I found through all of those experiences is that I love solving problems, fixing things that are broken, and serving my community. Being a handyman gives me the opportunity to use my tools, experience, and abilities to do those things.

My wife Olivia and I live in Stanwood/Camano in a tiny house that we built together. I look forward to working with you!
